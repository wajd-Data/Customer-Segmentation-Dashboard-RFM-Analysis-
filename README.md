# Customer Segmentation Dashboard (RFM Analysis)

## Overview

This project focuses on customer segmentation using the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) model to analyze customer purchasing behavior.

Using SQL for data preparation and Power BI for visualization, this dashboard helps identify valuable customers, customers at risk of churn, and revenue-driving segments.

The goal of this project is to provide actionable insights into customer behavior and support data-driven business decisions.

---

 ## RFM Model Explanation

RFM stands for:

- Recency (R): How recently a customer made a purchase
- Frequency (F): How often a customer makes purchases
- Monetary (M): How much money a customer spends

Customers were scored based on these metrics and categorized into meaningful segments such as:

- Loyal Customers
- At Risk Customers
- New Customers
- Potential Customers
- Lost Customers
- Champions

## Key Insights

Some example insights from the analysis:

- Loyal customers generate the highest revenue.
- At-risk customers show high recency values, indicating potential churn risk.
- New and potential customers have low frequency scores, suggesting opportunities for targeted marketing.
- Customer segmentation enables businesses to focus retention and marketing strategies effectively.
